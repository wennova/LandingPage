How Our Project Works

  • #1

    Submit Your Request

    Go to the Join Our Project form, and share your interest with us. Note: Submitting your request doesn’t mean you have entered the project.

  • #2

    Submission Analysis

    After receiving your submission, we will analyze if your company fits on the Wennova project. We will contact you by email provided.

  • #3

    Commitment Agreement

    After your submission's approval, we will book a virtual meeting to share the reg &rules and sign the commitment agreement.

  • #4

    Diagnostic Phase

    After collecting more details from your company, we will start the diagnostic and present what areas will be our priority during the project's three months.
