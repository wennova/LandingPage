WHY WENNOVA

Wennova is a volunteer project created by professionals who are willing to share their expertise to support small businesses affected by covid-19. Check below how we can help you to grow in difficult times:

Business Strategy

Do you have an opportunity or a new endeavor? A problem or potential improvement? We can help you to analyze your business, fallowing 8 easy steps to get the resultingÂ impact wished.

Marketing / Sales

From creating a website to developing a campaign from start to finish, our team will analyze your marketing funnel and help you to understand what are the next steps to succeed in the digital world.

Web Development

Don't you have a digital presence? Our team can help you to develop or to improve a functional website that will help you perform and generate digital visibility for your company.
